What is a BOQ?

A Bill of Quantities (BOQ) is a complete list of items, quantities, and rates needed to execute a project. It's essential for budgeting and tendering.

How accurate are these estimates?

Our app produces preliminary budget estimates suitable for feasibility and planning. All quantities should be verified by a licensed engineer or QS before final tender.

Which building codes are supported?

We support SBC 304 (Saudi), IBC/ACI (USA), EC2 (Europe), IS 456 (India), AS 3600 (Australia), and a generic international standard.

Can I export my BOQ?

Yes! Pro users can export their BOQ as PDF or Excel for sharing with clients, consultants, and contractors.
