Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Summary in one sentence: BOQ Quick Estimator stores all your data locally on your own device only. We collect nothing, store nothing on servers, and share nothing with anyone.

1. What data we collect

BOQ Quick Estimator collects no personal data. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or contact information
  • We do not collect project names, client names, or any construction data you enter
  • We do not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data
  • We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, or third-party monitoring tools
  • We do not use cookies

2. Where your data is stored

All data you enter into BOQ Quick Estimator — project names, room dimensions, client information, and calculated quantities — is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage on your own device.

  • Data never leaves your device
  • Data is never transmitted to any server
  • Data is never accessible to us or any third party
  • You can delete all stored data at any time by clearing your browser data

3. Access codes and payments

When you purchase Pro access, the transaction occurs through a third-party platform (WhatsApp, STC Pay, or bank transfer). We receive only what you voluntarily share during that transaction (your name and WhatsApp number) to send you your access code. This information is stored locally by us only for order management and is never shared with any third party.

Access codes are verified locally in your browser. Your access code is stored in your browser's sessionStorage and is not transmitted to any server.

4. Third-party services

The app loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). Google may log this request according to their own privacy policy. No other third-party services are used.

5. Children's privacy

BOQ Quick Estimator is a professional construction tool intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

6. Changes to this policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "last updated" date.
