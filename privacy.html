Last updated: March 2026 · BOQ Quick Estimator

Short version: Your construction project data stays entirely on your device. We collect your contact details (name, email, phone) only when you register for access. We never sell your data to third parties.

1. Who we are

BOQ Quick Estimator is a professional construction estimating tool operated by Al Qaswaa Al Sharqia Trading EST, an EPC contractor based in the Eastern Province of Saudi Arabia. Contact: info@qaswasharqia.com · +966 51 175 9092

2. What data we collect

We collect the following when you register for access:

  • Name — to personalise your access code and communication
  • Email address — for account support and service updates
  • Phone / WhatsApp number — to deliver your access code and provide support
  • Role / purpose — to understand how the tool is used and improve it

We do not collect your project data, drawings, room dimensions, or any construction information you enter into the app. All of that stays on your device.

3. How we use your data

  • To send your access code via WhatsApp after payment
  • To provide technical support when requested
  • To notify you of important updates to the app
  • To understand user demographics and improve the product

4. Where your project data lives

All BOQ estimates, cost plans, room data and project information you create in the app are stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage — on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

5. Cookies and analytics

BOQ Quick Estimator does not use cookies or any third-party analytics tracking. No advertising pixels, tracking scripts, or external data collection tools are used.

6. Data sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your registration data (name, email, phone) is held privately by Al Qaswaa Al Sharqia Trading EST and used solely for service delivery.

7. WhatsApp communication

By providing your WhatsApp number during registration, you consent to receiving your access code and occasional service-related messages via WhatsApp. You may opt out at any time by messaging us.

8. Data retention

Registration data is retained for the duration of your active subscription plus 12 months. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us on WhatsApp or email.

9. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of your personal data we hold
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact: info@qaswasharqia.com or WhatsApp +966 51 175 9092

10. Security

As all project data is stored locally on your device, it is protected by your device's own security. Registration data held by us is stored securely. We do not store payment information — payments are processed via STC Pay or bank transfer directly.

11. Children

BOQ Quick Estimator is a professional tool intended for adults (18+). We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
