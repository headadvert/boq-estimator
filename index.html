FAQ

Common questions.

Are the quantities accurate enough for tendering? +
BOQ Quick Estimator produces preliminary budget quantities suitable for early-stage costing, client budgeting and feasibility. All quantities should be verified by a licensed structural engineer or quantity surveyor before final tender. The app clearly states this on every export.
Where is my data stored? +
Everything stays on your device. Projects are saved in your browser's local storage — nothing is sent to any server. This means your client data is completely private and you can use the app with no internet connection after the first load.
Which building code should I use for my country? +
Select the code that governs construction in your jurisdiction. Saudi Arabia uses SBC 304, UAE uses IBC/ACI, the UK uses Eurocode 2 (formerly BS 8110), the US uses ACI 318, Europe uses Eurocode 2, India uses IS 456, and Australia uses AS 3600. If your country isn't listed, select International for generic industry-average factors.
Can I use this on my phone on-site? +
Yes. The app is fully responsive and works on any device — phone, tablet or desktop. It also works offline once loaded, so you can use it anywhere on site without a data connection.
What payment methods are accepted? +
Payment is processed securely via Stripe. All major credit and debit cards are accepted including Visa, Mastercard and American Express. Apple Pay and Google Pay are available depending on your device.
Is this a subscription? +
No. The Pro plan is a one-time payment of $19 for lifetime access including all future updates. You will never be charged again.
