Common questions

FAQ

Are the quantities accurate enough for tendering?+

BOQ Quick Estimator produces preliminary budget quantities suitable for early-stage costing, client budgeting and feasibility. All quantities should be verified by a licensed structural engineer or quantity surveyor before final tender. The app clearly states this on every export.

What does CAD-assisted entry mean?+

Since full DWG parsing requires a desktop application, CAD-assisted entry lets you upload your DWG, DXF, or PDF drawing to register the file, then enter the key dimensions (floor areas, height) which you read from your drawing. The app applies all code-correct factors automatically — saving the manual calculation work while using your actual drawing data.

What MEP systems are included?+

Electrical installation (wiring, DBs, sockets, lighting, ELV), plumbing & drainage (supply pipes, sanitary fittings, hot/cold water), HVAC / air conditioning (split or central plant, ductwork, controls), and fire & safety systems (sprinklers, detectors, alarms, hose reels, signage). All are calculated as provisional sums per your selected code and project type, then fully priced in the Cost Estimator.

Where is my data stored?+

Everything stays on your device. Projects are saved in your browser's local storage — nothing is sent to any server. Your client data is completely private and you can use the app offline after the first load.

Can I use this on my phone on-site?+

Yes. The app is fully responsive and works on any device — phone, tablet or desktop. It also works offline once loaded, so you can use it anywhere on site without a data connection.
