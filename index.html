Simple workflow

in four steps.

01

Choose project type and building code

Select from 6 project types and 8 building codes. All quantities, MEP systems and unit rates auto-calibrate to your selection. Start with residential or commercial — it matters for MEP scope.

Enter dimensions or upload your drawing

Add rooms manually (L × W × H, doors, windows) or use CAD-assisted entry to upload your DWG or PDF drawing. The app reads the file and guides you through confirming each floor area.

Common questions

FAQ

What is the difference between monthly and annual plans?+

The monthly plan is SAR 55/month (cancel anytime). The annual plan is SAR 480/year — equivalent to SAR 40/month, saving you SAR 180 (27%) compared to monthly billing. Annual subscribers also get early access to new tools and feature updates. Both plans include full Pro access to both apps, unlimited projects, and PDF export.

Are the quantities accurate enough for tendering?+

BOQ Quick Estimator produces preliminary budget quantities suitable for early-stage costing, client budgeting and feasibility. All quantities should be verified by a licensed structural engineer or quantity surveyor before final tender. The app clearly states this on every export.

What MEP systems are calculated?+

Four MEP systems are included: (1) Electrical installation — wiring, distribution boards, sockets, lighting and ELV; (2) Plumbing & drainage — supply pipes, sanitary fittings, hot/cold water; (3) HVAC / air conditioning — split or central plant, ductwork, controls; (4) Fire & safety — sprinklers, detectors, alarms, hose reels and signage. All are calculated per your selected building code and project type.

How does CAD-assisted entry work?+

Upload your DWG, DXF or PDF drawing. The app registers the file and guides you through entering the key dimensions (floor areas, heights) which you read from your drawing. All code-correct calculation factors are then applied automatically — saving the calculation work while using your actual drawing data as the source.

How do I pay and receive access?+

Payment is accepted via STC Pay or bank transfer (IBAN). After confirming your payment, we send you a unique access code on WhatsApp within 1 hour. Enter the code at the access page to unlock the full Pro app.

Where is my data stored?+

Everything stays on your device. Projects save in your browser's local storage — nothing is sent to any server. Your client data is completely private and the app works offline after the first load.

Can I cancel the monthly plan anytime?+

Yes. The monthly plan runs month-to-month. To cancel, simply stop renewing — no contracts, no cancellation fees. Your access remains active until the end of the paid period. Annual plans are billed once per year and are non-refundable after the first month.
